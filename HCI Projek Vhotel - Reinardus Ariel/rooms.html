— Rooms —

Standard room

Standard hotel room uses single bed with size 38 inches x 75 inches and one bathroom.

Superior room

Superior type rooms are slightly more spacious, and although the room facilities are the same as standard types. Superior Room is divided into 2 types of beds: Twin bed and double bed.

Deluxe room

Deluxe Room is a type of room above the superior. Deluxe room is more spacious than superior, around 40 square meters.

Family room

This family room is a type of room that has two double beds or one double bed and one single bed. So, one room can fit 3-4 people, you no longer need to separate rooms with your family.

Luxurious room

Luxurious room is the highest type of room where there are 3 bedroom and a kitchen.
