ABOUT

Vhotel
The Vhotel was built in 1999 and since then has become one of the iconic hotels in the city. vhotel was founded with a vision to create a special and satisfying stay experience for hotel guests. With this vision, Vhotel was established as a place to stay that combines luxury, hospitality, and a touch of history. vhotel is located in Jakarta and has gone through various changes and restorations to restore its original beauty. Vhotel continues to innovate and update its services and facilities to meet the expectations of modern guests. Despite being in operation for many years, the Vhotel remains the go-to destination for those seeking a special stay experience with a priceless touch of history.
